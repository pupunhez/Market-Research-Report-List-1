<p><strong>The Automotive Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive castings market features several die casting techniques, each suited for specific applications. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten metal under high pressure into a mold, producing precise parts quickly. Vacuum die casting eliminates air from the mold, enhancing surface finish and mechanical properties. Squeeze die casting exerts pressure from both sides to improve material density and accuracy. Semi-solid die casting uses a partially solidified metal, offering better control over shape and reduced porosity, enhancing performance in automotive components.</p></p>
