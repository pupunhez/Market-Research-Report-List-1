<p><p>The industrial dryers market comprises three primary types: direct dryers, indirect dryers, and specialty dryers. Direct dryers, such as air or gas-fired designs, utilize heated air or gases to remove moisture from materials. Indirect dryers apply heat through a medium without direct contact, ensuring gentle drying suitable for sensitive materials. Specialty dryers cater to specific industries or applications, such as freeze dryers for pharmaceutical products or rotary dryers for bulk solids, addressing unique drying requirements and enhancing efficiency.</p></p>
