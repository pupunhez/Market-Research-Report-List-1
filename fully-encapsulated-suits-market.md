<p><p>The fully encapsulated suits market is divided into two main types: fully encapsulating suits and partially encapsulating suits. Fully encapsulating suits provide complete protection against hazardous materials, covering the entire body with airtight seals and layers to prevent contamination. In contrast, partially encapsulating suits offer a degree of protection, covering major areas while allowing for some exposure. These suits are utilized in various industries, including healthcare, military, and chemical, depending on the level of risk and required safety measures.</p></p>
