<p><p>The copper-coated wire market for welding includes three main types: Copper Coated SAW Wire, Copper Coated MIG Wire, and Copper Coated TIG Wire. Copper Coated SAW Wire is used in submerged arc welding, providing excellent electrical conductivity and weld quality. Copper Coated MIG Wire is ideal for gas metal arc welding, offering smooth feeding and superior weld appearance. Copper Coated TIG Wire is designed for tungsten inert gas welding, ensuring strong, clean welds in various materials. Each type enhances welding performance and efficiency.</p></p>
