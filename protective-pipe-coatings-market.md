<p><p>The protective pipe coatings market is divided into internal and external coatings, each serving distinct purposes. Internal coatings are applied to the inner surfaces of pipes to enhance flow efficiency, prevent corrosion, and resist chemical exposure, thus extending the lifespan of the pipes. External coatings, on the other hand, protect the outer surfaces from environmental factors, moisture, UV radiation, and physical damage. Both types are crucial for maintaining the integrity and performance of piping systems across various industries.</p></p>
