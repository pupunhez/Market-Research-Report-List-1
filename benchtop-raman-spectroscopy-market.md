<p><p>Benchtop Raman Spectroscopy encompasses two primary market types: invasive and non-invasive Raman spectrometers. Invasive Raman spectrometers physically interact with samples, often requiring sample preparation or direct engagement, which can provide detailed analysis but may alter the sample's state. In contrast, non-invasive Raman spectrometers allow for analysis without disturbing the sample, ideal for delicate materials or real-time monitoring. This differentiation enhances their applications across various fields, including pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and materials science, catering to diverse research and industrial needs.</p></p>
