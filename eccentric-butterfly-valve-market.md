<p><p>The eccentric butterfly valve market is categorized into three main types: single, double, and triple eccentric valves. Single eccentric valves have an offset axis which allows for improved sealing and reduced friction. Double eccentric valves feature a second offset, further enhancing performance and functionality by minimizing wear during operation. Triple eccentric valves incorporate a third offset, allowing for tight sealing under high-pressure conditions and high-temperature applications, making them ideal for demanding environments. Each type offers distinct advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
