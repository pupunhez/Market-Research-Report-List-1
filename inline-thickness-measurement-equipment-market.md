<p><p>Inline thickness measurement equipment is essential for various industries, ensuring product quality and compliance. The market is primarily segmented into three types: ultrasonic, eddy current, and others. Ultrasonic devices utilize sound waves to gauge thickness by measuring time delays, making them suitable for diverse materials. Eddy current techniques employ electromagnetic induction to assess thickness, particularly in conductive materials. The "others" category includes technologies like laser and optical measurement systems, each offering unique advantages depending on application requirements and material properties.</p></p>
