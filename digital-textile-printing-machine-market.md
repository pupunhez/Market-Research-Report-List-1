<p><strong>The Digital Textile Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-to-garment</li><li>Direct-to-fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital textile printing machine market is primarily divided into two types: direct-to-garment (DTG) and direct-to-fabric (DTF). DTG printing is focused on applying designs directly onto garments, making it ideal for small runs and intricate patterns with vibrant colors. In contrast, DTF targets printing on fabric rolls before they are cut and sewn, allowing for greater versatility and faster production for larger-scale applications. Both methods offer high-quality prints while reducing waste and enabling customization.</p></p>
