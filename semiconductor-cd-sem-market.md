<p><strong>The Semiconductor CD SEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photomask CD-SEM</li><li>Wafer CD-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor CD-SEM (Critical Dimension Scanning Electron Microscopy) market is divided into two key types: Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM. Photomask CD-SEM focuses on measuring critical dimensions on photomasks, which are essential for defining microchip patterns, ensuring precision in fabrication. Wafer CD-SEM, on the other hand, is used for measuring critical dimensions on wafers during semiconductor production, providing crucial feedback for process control and optimization. Both types are vital for maintaining the integrity and performance of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
