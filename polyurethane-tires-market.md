<p><strong>The Polyurethane Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPG Polyurethane</li><li>PTMEG Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane tires market comprises primarily two types: PPG polyurethane and PTMEG polyurethane. PPG polyurethane utilizes polyether polyols from PPG Industries, offering durability and resistance to wear, ideal for various vehicle applications. In contrast, PTMEG polyurethane employs polytetramethylene ether glycol, providing excellent elasticity and resilience, making it suitable for high-performance tires. Both types enhance tire performance, energy efficiency, and longevity, catering to the demands of automotive and industrial sectors while addressing environmental concerns through improved formulations.</p></p>
