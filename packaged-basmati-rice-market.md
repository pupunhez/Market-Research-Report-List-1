<p><p>The packaged Basmati rice market is primarily segmented into two types: dry Basmati rice and parboiled Basmati rice. Dry Basmati rice is typically unprocessed, preserving its natural flavor and aroma, and is favored for traditional cooking methods. Parboiled Basmati rice, on the other hand, undergoes a partial boiling process before milling, enhancing its nutritional value and reducing cooking time. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with dry rice appealing to purists and parboiled rice appealing to those seeking convenience.</p></p>
