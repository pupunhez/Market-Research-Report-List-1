<p><strong>The Energy and Sport Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy and sports drinks market is segmented into three main types: hypotonic, isotonic, and hypertonic sports drinks. Hypotonic drinks contain low levels of carbohydrates and electrolytes, making them ideal for rapid rehydration post-exercise. Isotonic drinks have balanced carbohydrate and electrolyte concentrations, effectively replenishing fluid and energy during workouts. Hypertonic drinks, with a higher concentration of carbs, serve as energy boosters but require additional water for rehydration, primarily suited for endurance athletes after intense activities.</p></p>
