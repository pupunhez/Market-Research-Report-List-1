<p><p>The nanoscale hollow tubes market is primarily categorized into single-walled and multi-walled tubes. Single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWCNTs) consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical shape, offering unique electrical and thermal properties, ideal for advanced electronic applications. Multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs), on the other hand, contain multiple concentric layers of carbon, providing greater mechanical strength and enhanced conductivity, making them suitable for composite materials and energy storage solutions. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
