<p><p>The automotive rearview mirror market comprises several types, each designed for specific visibility needs. Flat mirrors offer a standard view but can create blind spots. Convex mirrors provide a wider field of vision, reducing blind spots at the expense of distance accuracy. Aspheric mirrors combine flat and convex elements, enhancing peripheral vision while minimizing distortion. Wide-angle mirrors further expand visibility, improving safety by allowing drivers to see more of their surroundings, particularly in busy driving conditions.</p></p>
