<p><p>The single dose laundry detergent pods market is divided primarily into two types: non-bio and bio laundry detergents. Non-bio laundry detergents are formulated without enzymes, making them gentler on sensitive skin and ideal for washing delicate fabrics. They effectively tackle stains through surfactants and other cleaning agents. In contrast, bio laundry detergents are enzyme-based, providing enhanced stain removal capabilities, especially for protein-based stains like food and sweat. This differentiation caters to diverse consumer preferences and laundry needs in the market.</p></p>
