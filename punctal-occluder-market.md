<p><strong>The Punctal Occluder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dissolvable</li><li>Dissolvable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The punctal occluder market is divided into two main types: non-dissolvable and dissolvable occluders. Non-dissolvable occluders are designed to provide a long-term solution by blocking tear drainage through the puncta, typically made from materials like silicone or acrylic. In contrast, dissolvable occluders are temporary, made from biodegradable materials that gradually dissolve over time, allowing for controlled tear drainage restoration. Both types serve to alleviate dry eye symptoms by enhancing tear film stability and improving ocular comfort.</p></p>
