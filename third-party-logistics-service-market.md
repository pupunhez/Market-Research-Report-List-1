<p><strong>The Third Party Logistics Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asset Based Third Party Logistics Service</li><li>Non-Asset Based Third Party Logistics Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Third Party Logistics (3PL) Service market is categorized into two primary types: Asset-Based and Non-Asset Based services. Asset-Based 3PL providers own the physical assets, such as warehouses and transportation vehicles, allowing them to offer direct logistics solutions. In contrast, Non-Asset Based 3PL services do not own these assets but instead utilize external carriers and facilities, focusing on management and coordination of logistics activities. Both types enhance supply chain efficiency, but differ in ownership and operational flexibility.</p></p>
