<p><p>The Fabric Water Shield market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based formulations. Water-based shields utilize water as a solvent, offering a more environmentally friendly option with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them safer for indoor use. In contrast, solvent-based shields contain organic solvents, providing superior durability and resistance to harsh weather conditions, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Both types serve to enhance fabric performance by providing water repellency and protecting against stains and moisture damage.</p></p>
