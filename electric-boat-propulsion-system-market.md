<p><p>The electric boat propulsion system market is primarily divided into two types: All Electric and Hybrid Electric. All Electric systems utilize solely electric power for propulsion, relying on batteries or fuel cells, resulting in zero emissions and quieter operation. In contrast, Hybrid Electric systems combine traditional combustion engines with electric propulsion, providing flexibility and extended range. This dual approach allows for reduced fuel consumption and emissions while enhancing performance, making both options appealing for environmentally conscious boating enthusiasts.</p></p>
