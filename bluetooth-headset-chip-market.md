<p><ul><li>TWS Bluetooth Headset Chip</li><li>Non-TWS Bluetooth Headset Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth headset chip market is primarily divided into two types: TWS (True Wireless Stereo) Bluetooth headset chips and Non-TWS Bluetooth headset chips. TWS chips enable fully wireless earbuds, enhancing user convenience and mobility with features like seamless pairing and stereo sound. In contrast, Non-TWS chips are commonly used in traditional wired or Bluetooth headsets that maintain a wired connection between the earpieces or devices, often focusing on delivering reliable audio quality and extended battery life without the true wireless experience.</p></p>
