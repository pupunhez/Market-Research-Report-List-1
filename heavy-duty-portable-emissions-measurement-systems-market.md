<p><p>The Heavy Duty Portable Emissions Measurement Systems (PEMS) market consists of two main types: Real-Driving Emissions (RDE) PEMS and Integrated PEMS (iPEMS). RDE PEMS are designed for measuring emissions during real-world driving conditions, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. In contrast, iPEMS combine various measurement technologies into a single platform, offering enhanced data collection and processing capabilities. Both types support the automotive industry's shift towards more stringent emissions standards and contribute to improved air quality by monitoring vehicle performance under actual operating conditions.</p></p>
