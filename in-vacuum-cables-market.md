<p><p>In the vacuum cables market, two primary types are recognized: High In-Vacuum Cables and UHV (Ultra High Voltage) In-Vacuum Cables. High In-Vacuum Cables are designed for applications requiring reliable performance in low-pressure environments, providing effective insulation and heat resistance. UHV In-Vacuum Cables, on the other hand, are engineered to handle extremely high voltage levels, ensuring enhanced safety and efficiency. Both types cater to industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, particle accelerators, and advanced research facilities.</p></p>
