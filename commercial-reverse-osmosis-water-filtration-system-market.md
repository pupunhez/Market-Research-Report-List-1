<p><p>The Commercial Reverse Osmosis Water Filtration System market includes "Under Sink RO Systems" and "Traditional RO Systems." Under Sink RO Systems are compact units installed beneath sinks, providing purified water directly from a dedicated tap, ideal for space-constrained environments. Traditional RO Systems, however, are larger and typically installed in a central location, catering to higher water demand in commercial settings. Both types effectively remove contaminants, but their installation and capacity differ, influencing their suitability for various applications.</p></p>
