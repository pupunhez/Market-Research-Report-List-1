<p><strong>The Durometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brinell Durometer</li><li>Vickers Durometer</li><li>Rockwell Durometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The durometer market comprises various hardness measurement types, primarily categorized into Brinell, Vickers, Rockwell, and others. Brinell durometers assess hardness through a hard sphere indenter and are useful for larger materials. Vickers durometers utilize a diamond pyramid for precise measurements across materials, suitable for both soft and hard substances. Rockwell durometers provide quick and reliable hardness readings, emphasizing different scales for varying depths. Other types include Shore and Mohs durometers, catering to specific industry needs and material applications.</p></p>
