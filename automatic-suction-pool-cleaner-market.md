<p><strong>The Automatic Suction Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Robotic Type</li><li>Suction-side Type</li><li>Pressure-side Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic suction pool cleaner market encompasses three main types: robotic, suction-side, and pressure-side cleaners. </p><p>Robotic cleaners operate independently, using motors and sensors to navigate and clean efficiently. Suction-side cleaners connect to the pool's filtration system, relying on suction power to remove debris. Pressure-side cleaners use water pressure from the pump to dislodge dirt and debris, typically collecting it in a separate bag. Each type offers distinct cleaning methods and efficiencies, catering to various pool maintenance needs and user preferences.</p></p>
