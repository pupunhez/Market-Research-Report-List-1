<p><strong>The Defibrillator Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator</li><li>External Cardioverter Defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillator devices market includes two primary types: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) and External Cardioverter Defibrillators. ICDs are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when life-threatening arrhythmias are detected, offering long-term protection for at-risk patients. In contrast, External Cardioverter Defibrillators are portable devices used in emergency situations to deliver shocks from outside the body, usually employed by medical professionals or trained bystanders to restore normal heart rhythms during cardiac events.</p></p>
