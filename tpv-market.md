<p><strong>The TPV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM/PP Blends</li><li>NR/PP Blends</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TPV (Thermoplastic Vulcanizate) market encompasses various blends, including EPDM/PP and NR/PP blends. EPDM/PP blends combine ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber with polypropylene, offering excellent weather resistance and flexibility, ideal for automotive and construction applications. NR/PP blends merge natural rubber with polypropylene, providing enhanced elasticity and impact resistance, suitable for consumer goods and industrial uses. Both blends enhance the properties of thermoplastics, catering to diverse industry needs, ensuring durability and versatility in final products.</p></p>
