<p><p>The domestic refrigerator and freezer market can be categorized into three main types: freezer on top, freezer on bottom, and freezer-less models. Freezer on top refrigerators feature a traditional design with the freezer compartment positioned above the fridge, promoting accessibility to frequently used refrigerated items. Freezer on bottom models offer easier access to frozen food, placing the freezer below the refrigerator. Freezer-less refrigerators, devoid of a freezing compartment, provide additional storage space and are ideal for users who rely more on fresh food.</p></p>
