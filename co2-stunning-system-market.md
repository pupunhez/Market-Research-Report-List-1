<p><ul><li>Continuous Gas System</li><li>Disposable Gas Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 stunning system market is primarily categorized into continuous gas systems and disposable gas systems. Continuous gas systems utilize a steady supply of CO2, ensuring a constant and controlled stunning process, often preferred in larger operations for efficiency. In contrast, disposable gas systems use pre-packaged gas sources for each stunning event, offering convenience and hygiene, particularly suitable for smaller facilities or situations requiring mobility. Both types aim to improve animal welfare by ensuring a humane method of stunning before slaughter.</p></p>
